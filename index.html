Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) Course is rapidly growing fields in computer science that are revolutionizing the way we interact with technology. AI involves creating intelligent systems that can perform tasks that typically require human-like intelligence. ML, on the other hand, is a subset of AI that involves developing algorithms and models that can learn from data and make predictions or decisions based on that learning.

Our Journey

Our exciting journey began in 2021, with a sanctioned student strength of 60. The AIML Department proudly boasts that 50% of the faculty members have either completed or are pursuing their Ph.D., guiding seven research scholars in their respective areas of expertise.

Student Association

The department also has a unique student association, "pAIthon," which actively organizes technical, co-curricular, and cultural activities to ensure the overall development of students.
