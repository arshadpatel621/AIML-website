Mission & Vision

We shape responsible technologists with a strong foundation in AI who contribute to society through innovation and ethics.

Vision

To be a premier center of excellence in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning through interdisciplinary partnerships and by making positive contributions to the community, organizations, and society as a whole.

Mission

The mission of the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Bidar, is to:

  • M1: Impart knowledge in cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence technologies in line with industrial standards.
  • M2: Promote innovative research and development in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and serve the needs of society through collaboration with industries and allied organizations.
  • M3: Produce successful engineering graduates specialized in AI/ML who uphold personal, professional, and ethical responsibilities, with a commitment to lifelong learning.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • PSO 1: Apply concepts from core areas of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning — such as Data Structures, Database Systems, Operating Systems, Networking, and Intelligent Systems — to solve real-world and future-oriented problems.
  • PSO 2: Develop automated solutions for real-world problems through laboratory experiments, projects, and internships.
