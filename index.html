About Us

The AIML Department fosters a vibrant learning ecosystem focused on cutting-edge AI, machine learning, and data-driven innovation. With modern labs, dedicated faculty, and industry collaborations, we prepare students to solve real-world problems.

Why AIML?

Interdisciplinary curriculum, research-driven projects, hackathons, and internships with leading companies ensure practical exposure and strong career outcomes.

  • Outcome-based education
  • Industry mentors and guest lectures
  • Capstone and research projects

Facilities

State-of-the-art labs equipped with GPUs, collaborative classrooms, and access to cloud resources for scalable model training and deployment.

  • AI & DL Lab
  • Data Science Lab
  • Cloud & MLOps Suite

Mission & Vision
